Part 1. Write the situation from dictation.

A mixed Academic Exam and Soft Skills lesson in 3 parts.

So, now you've studied some questions think about the tone you'll need to use (respectful to a professor, tactful and semi-formal with a business, tactful but friendly with a friend). This task has been added to PTE in response to criticism about the lack of soft skills so your tone of voice and choice of words will be more important than a perfectly remembered stock answer. The human examiners are looking for spontaneity and suitable language for a specific situation.
